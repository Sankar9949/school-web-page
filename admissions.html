Admission Process

  1. Collect the application form from the school office or complete the form below.
  2. Submit the form with the required documents.
  3. Attend a student interaction or assessment, if applicable.
  4. Receive confirmation from the admissions office.
  5. Pay the fees and complete enrolment.

Required Documents

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Previous school report card
  • Transfer certificate, if applicable
  • Copy of Aadhaar card
